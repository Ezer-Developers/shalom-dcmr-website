About

Shalom D.C.M.R: Nurturing Special Minds Since 1985

Welcome to Shalom D.C.M.R, a beacon of hope and progress in the world of special education. Since our inception in January 1985, we have been dedicated to the empowerment and holistic development of children with special needs. As a unit of the Ashanilayam Social Service Centre, we stand registered under Act XII of 1955 (Registration No: K 217/1978, dated 9-8-1978) and are located in the serene Puliyannoor village. Our foundation lies in the compassionate and skilled hands of the Franciscan Clarist Congregation, Alphonsa Province Pala (FCC), in the Diocese of Pala.
