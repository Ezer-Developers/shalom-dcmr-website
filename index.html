About

Shalom D.C.M.R: Nurturing Special Minds Since 1985

Shalom D.C.M.R a special school for the Mentally Retarded children was started on January 1985 , as a unit of Ashanilayam Social , Service Centre , a charitable society, registered under Act XII of 1955 bearing the registration No: K 2I7 / 1978, dated 9-8-1978.The special school is located in the puliyannoor village.( Phone -04822-206138 ., 8086031082) It is run by the Sisters of Franciscan clarist Congreation, Alphonsa Province Pala (FCC) in the Diocese of pala. Shalom D.C.MR special school working under the Registration the Govt. of Kerala (General Education Department).

What we do

We treat our stundents with
best of our LOVE!

Specialized Education

Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to address the specific learning requirements of each child. We employ specialized teaching methods and strategies to ensure that every student receives the individualized attention they deserve.

Speech Therapy

We offer speech therapy services to help children improve their communication skills, overcome speech impediments, and enhance their ability to express themselves effectively.

Occupational Therapy

Our occupational therapy programs focus on developing essential life skills, fine motor skills, and sensory integration to promote independence and self-sufficiency.

Recreational Activities

We believe in the importance of holistic development. Our students engage in recreational activities that promote physical fitness, creativity, and social interaction.

Supportive Environment

Shalom D.C.M.R provides a loving and inclusive atmosphere where children feel safe, secure, and cared for. Our dedicated staff ensures that each child experiences a sense of belonging and self-worth.

Family-Centric Approach

We actively involve parents and guardians in their child's educational journey. Regular meetings, counseling sessions, and workshops are conducted to provide families with the tools and knowledge to support their children effectively.

Community Outreach

Beyond our school walls, we engage in community outreach programs, awareness campaigns, and collaborations with other organizations to create a more inclusive society for individuals with special needs.

Physiotherapy

Shalom D.C.M.R offers physiotherapy services to enhance the physical well-being of the children. These sessions are designed to improve mobility, strength, and coordination, ensuring that each child can develop their physical abilities to the fullest.
